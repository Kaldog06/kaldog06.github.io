Instructions

1. If you have played Minecraft 1.19.4 before, skip to step 11

2. Open Minecraft Launcher

3. Click on "Installations" on the top

4. Click "New Installation"

5. Set version to "release 1.19.4"

6. Press "Create" on the bottom right

7. Scroll to the bottom of your installations

8. Hover over the new installation

9. Press "Play" on the right

10. When the game has fully loaded, close it


11. Make sure your Minecraft Launcher is fully closed

12. Download the Fabric Installer from the link below

13. Run the Fabric Installer

14. Change the version to 1.19.4

15. When this is done, press OK

16. Close the installer


17. Open your Minecraft Launcher

18. Press the "Installations" tab on the top

19. While hovering over the new "fabric-loader-1.19.4" profile, press Play

20. When the game has finished loading completely, close it

21. Go back to the "fabric-loader-1.19.4" under "Installations"

22. Instead of pressing Play, click on the folder icon next to it

23. Open the mods folder

24. Download the "server mods.zip" from below

25. Right click the downloaded file and click "Extract All..."

26. Open the newly generated "server mods" folder

27. Copy EVERYTHING from this folder into the mods folder from step 23
