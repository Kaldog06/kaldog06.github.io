Instructions

1. Make sure your Minecraft Launcher is fully closed

2. Download the Fabric Installer from the link below

3. Run the Fabric Installer

4. Change the version to 1.19.4

5. When this is done, press OK

6. Close the installer

7. Open your Minecraft Launcher

8. Press the "Installations" tab on the top

9. While hovering over the new "fabric-loader-1.19.4" profile, press the folder icon next to play

10. Open the mods folder

11. Download the "mods.zip" from below

12. Right click the downloaded file and click "Extract All..."

13. Open the newly generated "mods" folder

14. Copy EVERYTHING from this folder into the minecraft mods folder from step 10
